Spanish Omelette

Spain Omelette is an omelette to which chopped potatoes are added.It is one of the best known and most emblematic dishes of Spanish cuisine, being a very popular product that can be found in almost any bar or restaurant in the country.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatoes
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes.
  2. Heat the frying pan and add a good extra virgin olive oil.
  3. Add the cut potatoes and leave to cook for approximately twenty to thirty minutes over a low heat.
  4. Once the potatoes are brown, drain them and put them to the bowl with the eggs.
  5. Heat olive oil in another frying pan and add the onion pieces. Fry until golden brown in colour, caramelised but not burnt. The onion will be done before the potatoes, so drain and add to the bowl with the beaten egg.
  6. We cook the mixture we have and leave it in the pan until it reaches our favourite point.
  7. Finally we turn it over with a large plate and let it heat up on the other side.

This is how easy it would be to make the potato omelette, don't hesitate to try it, you'll love it.
