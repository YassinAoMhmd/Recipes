Paella

Paella is a dish originating from Valencia (Spain). Its main ingredient is rice, usually accompanied by seafood, chicken, pulses and other foods.The container used to make paella is known as a paellera or paella pan. It is a kind of frying pan with handles.

Ingredients

  • Bomba rice
  • Free-range chicken
  • Rabbit
  • Flat green bean
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Sweet paprika
  • Crushed tomato
  • Saffron
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add the chicken and the rabbit in pieces.
  3. When the chicken and the rabbit is browned,we add the vegetables. .
  4. The pre-heated water is poured in and let it cook for 30 minutes.
  5. Add the rice.
  6. Add the saffron.
  7. Cook over high heat for about 7 to 8 minutes.
  8. When the rice begins to emerge, lower the heat and cook for a further 4 or 5 minutes.
  9. Then cook for a further 3 or 4 minutes over a very low or medium heat, depending on the amount of stock.
